MO Decision

MO Decision branches

✅ Approve — Completed (no action)

  • All documents valid & complete
  • No red flags detected
  • System marks reKYC as completed
  • Merchant notified via email + dashboard
  • Account continues normally — no further action required

⚠️ Need Resubmission

  • Documents incomplete or unclear
  • Information mismatch (e.g., business name, address)
  • MO writes specific feedback on what to fix
  • Merchant notified — clock continues (counts as partial response)
  • Goes back to merchant POV flow → resubmit loop

🔎 Escalate to Compliance

  • High-risk indicators (industry, geography, ownership structure)
  • Possible sanctions / PEP / adverse media hit
  • Beyond MO's authority threshold
  • Case routed to Compliance queue with MO notes
  • Merchant informed of extended review
